Openhab 2 - Binding KNX 2 installieren

https://community.openhab.org/t/how-to-install-a-2-x-binding-manually-in-this-case-knx/12581/41



- Datei "org.openhab.binding.knx-2.0.0-20170310.jar" in Verzeichnis /usr/share/openhab2/addons/ kopieren
- alts Binding KNX 1.9 deinstallieren

Konsole:
- sudo systemctl stop openhab2.service
- cd /usr/share/openhab2
- ./start.sh

Openhab Konsole:
- feature:install openhab-transport-serial
- feature:list | grep knx
- bundle:list -s | grep knx

Openhab -> neustarten

-> PaperUI: Configuration -> Bindings
